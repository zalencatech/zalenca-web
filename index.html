Frequently Asked

How long does it take to build an AI system?

+
Timelines depend on complexity. A chatbot can be ready in 3–7 days. Full SaaS products or custom AI systems typically take 2–6 weeks. We always agree on a clear timeline before starting.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

+
Not at all. We handle 100% of the technical work. You just need to know your business and goals — we explain everything in plain language throughout.

Can you integrate AI into my existing systems?

+
Absolutely. We integrate AI into Shopify, WordPress, Salesforce, HubSpot, or fully custom software. We connect and enhance what you already have without disruption.

Where are your offices?

+
We operate from three global offices — Scarborough, Canada; Dubai, UAE; and Pakistan — allowing us to serve clients across North America, the Middle East, and South Asia.

What happens after delivery?

+
We provide post-launch support, monitoring, and maintenance. We're your long-term AI partner — continuously optimizing and upgrading systems as your business scales.

Every project is custom-scoped based on complexity, features, integrations, and timeline. Prices range from $100 for simple automations up to $10,000+ for full enterprise AI transformations. Email zalencatech@gmail.com for a free quote within 24 hours.
